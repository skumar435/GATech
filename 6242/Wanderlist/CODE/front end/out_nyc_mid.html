TRIP ITINERARY

HEADING TO NEW YORK!






DAY 1

  • Times Square
    One of the biggest major commercial intersection, tourist destination, entertainment center and neighborhood in NYC.

  • Central Park
    Another iconic landmark in NYC, there are various things to do here, from sports to recreation.

  • Rockefeller Center
    Visit the Rockefeller Center, and be swept in by the art present across the area.

  • Chrysler Building
    Visit the Chrysler building and take in the view for yourself.

  • Madison Square Park
    Visit the Eleven Madison, or the Madison Square Park, or maybe just do both.



DAY 2

  • The High Bridge
    The Swindler Cove, The High Bridge, or the River Park, there is a lot to visit around here.

  • Flushing Meadows Corona Park
    Is host to multiple sports stadiums and museums. Take your pick.

  • Unisphere
    Or the sixth Avenue, hosts some of the most popular architecture in the city.

  • Morris-Jumel Mansion
    Learn about America's history at the Morris-Jumel Mansion Museum.

  • Bronx Zoo
    The Bronx Zoo and the Botanical Gardens. Its a day out in nature.



DAY 3

  • Manhattan Bridge
    Considered to be one of the most modern suspension bridges, decide for yourself when you take a look around it.

  • Brooklyn Bridge
    Take a walk across New York City's most popular tourist attraction.

  • Statue of Liberty National Monument
    Visit the monument which is considered to be a universal symbol of freedom and democracy.

  • Washington Square Park
    The Park is known as among the finest parks in NYC, host to the Washington Square Arch.

  • The Woolworth Building
    Be a part of the historic architecture when you take the Woolworth tour and learn about ancient skyscrapers.
