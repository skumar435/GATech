TRIP ITINERARY

HEADING TO MIAMI!






DAY 1

  • Bayfront Park
    Visit the Bayfront Park in Downtown Miami.

  • Marlins Park
    Take a tour of the Marlins Baseball Park, home to the Miami Marlins.

  • Vizcaya Museum & Gardens
    Visit the stunningly beautiful villa and gardens in the heart of Miami.

  • Jungle Island
    Visit Miami's premium eco-adventure theme park cum entertainment destination.

  • Margaret Pace Park
    Take a look at the Phillip and Patricia Frost Museum of Science located here.



DAY 2

  • Tropical Audubon Society
    Be a part of the bird conservation drive at the Tropical Audubon Society.

  • Alhambra Water Tower
    Visit the historical Alhambra Water Tower in Coral Gables.

  • Ingrahams Park
    Take a walk around Ingrahams Park in Coral Gables.

  • Venetian Pool
    Jump into the Venetian Pool, an aquatic facility unlike any other in the country.

  • Peacock Park
    Lose yourself in an intimate secret garden by the sea and partake in the local culinary creations.



DAY 3

  • Discovery Miami Beach
    There are a lot of things to do here, including a walk across the ocean.

  • The Wolfsonian-Florida International University
    Explore the Wolfsonian-FIU, a museum that explores the inventive and provocative character of the modern world.

  • The Bass
    Visit The Bass, Miami Beach;s contemporary art museum.

  • Miami Seaquarium
    Visit Florida's best aquarium in the Miami Seaquarium.

  • Crandon Park
    Visit the various ecosystems in Crandon Park during your time here.
