TRIP ITINERARY

HEADING TO LOS ANGELES!






DAY 1

  • Public Art - Gateway to Los Angeles
    Visit historic downtown Los Angeles, throough the heart of the Fashion District.

  • Hollyhock House
    Visit the centerpiece of the city's Barnsdall Art Park. There is also the Guiness World Records Museum nearby.

  • Angels Flight Railway
    Visit Angels Flight Railway, The Broad and the Walt Disney Concert Hall.

  • El Pueblo de Los Angeles Historical Monument
    Visit El Pueblo Historical Monument, considered the birthplace of LA.

  • Grand Central Market
    Be a part of the Grand Central Market, an iconic destination bringing together the LA culture cuisine.



DAY 2

  • Santa Monica State Beach
    Drive across the pacific coastline and soak in the beauty of Santa Monica.

  • The Museum of Jurassic Technology
    Visit the Museum of Jurassic Technology. It is not what it sounds like.

  • Jerome C. Daniel Overlook above the Hollywood Bowl
    Sit at a hillside spot and take in the views for the ocean and the Hollywood sign itself.

  • 6000 Santa Monica Blvd
    Take a studio tour of the Paramount Studio or visit the Hollywood Forever Cemetery.

  • The Venice Beach Boardwalk
    It's exactly what it says. A walk across the ocean at the Venice beach.



DAY 3

  • Korean Friendship Bell
    Take a look at the Korean Friendship Bell and other cultural landmarks around the place.

  • Battleship USS Iowa Museum
    Visit the Battleship USS Iowa Museum and indulge in some naval history of the US.

  • Lake Hollywood Park
    Take a walk in the Lake Hollywood park which also overlooks the Hollywood sign. Never a bad idea.

  • Madame Tussauds Hollywood
    It never hurts to visit Hollywood once more. This time, take a look around Madam Tussauds.
