TRIP ITINERARY

HEADING TO PHILADELPHIA!






DAY 1

  • Old City Hall
    Walk around Philadelphia's historic Chestnut street and learn about the history associated with it.

  • Eastern State Penitentiary
    Visit the Eastern State Penitentiary, where the likes of Al Capone were held.

  • Wagner Free Institute of Science
    Explore the Wagner Free Institute of Science of Philadelphia.

  • One Liberty Observation Deck
    Go upto the One Liberty Observation Deck to take in stunning aerial views of the city.

  • The Rosenbach
    Take a tour through the historic house museum and a rare book library of the Rosenbach.



DAY 2

  • Fairmount Park
    Walk across the Fairmount Park, the largest municipal park in Philadelphia.

  • Lemon Hill Mansion
    Visit the Federal-style mansion in Fairmount Park, now operated as a house museum.

  • National Trust of Cliveden
    Walk across the various historical sites that are littered across the avenue and delve into the history of the city.

  • Laurel Hill Cemetery
    Visit the Laurel Hill Cemetery, one of the few cemeteries considered to be a National Historic Landmark.

  • Philadelphia Zoo
    Visit the Philadelphia Zoo. We do not need to say anything.



DAY 3

  • John Heinz National Wildlife Refuge
    Explore the John Heinz National Wildlife Refuge at Tinicum.

  • Fort Mifflin
    Visit The fort that withstood the greatest bombardment of the American Revolution.

  • Boathouse Row
    Visit Philadelphia's historic boathouses housing social and rowing clubs and their racing shells.
